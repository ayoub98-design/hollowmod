Descend into the Darkness

Forge your own path in Hollow Knight: Silksong! An epic action adventure through a vast ruined kingdom of insects and heroes. Explore twisting caverns, battle tainted creatures and befriend strange bugs, all in a classic, hand-drawn 2D style.

Game Features

Vast Interconnected World

Explore a massive underground kingdom of haunting ruins and strange creatures. The world of Hollow Knight: Silksong is open for you to discover at your own pace.

Challenging Combat

Face over 150 brutal foes and vanquish ancient knights. Master the art of combat and unlock powerful new skills and spells.

Hand-Drawn Art

Experience a world brought to life with traditional 2D frame-by-frame animation and a detailed, atmospheric art style.

Meet the Characters

The Knight

A silent, nameless warrior who ventures into Hallownest. Wields a nail as their primary weapon.

Hornet

A mysterious protector of Hallownest's ruins. Agile and deadly with her needle and thread.

Zote the Mighty

A wandering warrior with a high opinion of himself, despite his questionable combat skills.

The Pale King

The former ruler of Hallownest, whose actions set in motion the kingdom's downfall.
